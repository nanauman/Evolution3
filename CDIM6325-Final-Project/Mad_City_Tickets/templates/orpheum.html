About

Featured on the National Register of Historic Places this 1,700 seat venue, built in 1926, features some of the most popular national acts. Originally used as a movie theater and for vaudeville shows, this venue is now used for comedy, music, and variety shows. Located in Downtown Madison, one block away from the Capital, on the historic State Street. Address: 216 State St, Madison WI 53703
