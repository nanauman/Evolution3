About

Located in the heart of Downtown Madison's State Street. State Street Brats was established in 1989 with the goal of providing UW-Madison students with a dual purpose location where they can have lunch and a fun night out with their friends. Serving a variety of local taps and their in house made bratwurst State Street Brats is the place to be on State Street whether you are looking for a quick bite or a few beers. In additon, in the evenings State Street Brats hosts live ticketed concerts, featuring local bands and DJs. Address: 603 State St, Madison WI 53703
